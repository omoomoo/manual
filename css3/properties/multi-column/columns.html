columns

说明：本文档兼容性测试基础环境为：windows系统；IE6-IE10, Firefox9.0, Chrome16.0, Safari5.1, Opera11.60

语法

columns：column-width column-count

取值

取值 说明
column-width 设置列的宽度
column-count 设置列的数量

说明

默认值 auto auto
继承 no
版本 css3
Javascript脚本 object.style.columns="100px 3"

兼容性

示例

For the next three months, I didn’t just tinker around with blogging. I dedicated myself to it. I started work at 8 AM in the morning, and I kept going until 11 PM at night. I didn’t watch television. I didn’t see my friends.
